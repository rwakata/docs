**Xente API Documentation for Developers**

Xente's API is designed to automate payments and finance for your business, teams, and clients. 

This documentation will guide you through the various capabilities of our API and demonstrate how you can leverage it to streamline financial processes and enhance user experiences.


## Issue virtual and physical Visa debit cards

Easily issue virtual and physical Visa debit cards from your app that can be used to make payments online or at POS wherever Visa is accepted.

## Web Checkout

Accept payments from customers using mobile money and card payments with Xente's secure web checkout feature. Create a seamless payment experience for online transactions.

## Mobile Money Integration

Develop applications that facilitate single or bulk mobile money payments using Xente's API. Enable your customers to make payments directly from their mobile devices, ensuring a convenient and user-friendly experience.

## Airtime and Mobile Internet Purchases

Stay connected with Xente's API by building apps that facilitate airtime and mobile internet data purchases. Offer your users a hassle-free solution to keep their phones topped up.

## Bill Payments

Streamline bill payments for your users with Xente's API. Whether it's utilities, subscriptions, or other services, our API makes paying bills a quick and straightforward process.



## Collection of Mobile Money Payments

Integrate Xente into your app or website to collect payments directly from customers' mobile money accounts. This offers your customers a convenient and efficient payment method.

## Collection of Payments from Bank Accounts

Expand payment options by enabling payments from bank accounts with Xente's API. Provide your customers with more flexibility in how they pay.

## Collection of Payments through Agent Banking

Leverage agent banking networks to collect payments with Xente's API. Reach a wider audience and offer more payment channels to your customers.

## Syncing with Accounting and Line of Business Platforms

Integrate seamlessly with your existing accounting platforms using Xente. Our API enables you to synchronize payment data effortlessly, allowing you to streamline financial operations and ensure accurate records.

Xente's API provides you with a comprehensive suite of tools to automate payments, streamline financial processes, and enhance user experiences.
