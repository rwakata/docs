---
title: 'What is Xente'
description: 'Understand Xente and how it works'
---
Xente is a powerful software platform that businesses use to automate payment and finance operations. 
With Xente, you get access to a virtual bank account that allows you to 
- Issue virtual and physical Visa debit cards
- Make payments using mobile money, bank transfers, airtime, internet bundles, bill payments
- Collect payments via checkout, mobile money, bank transfers, and agent banking.

Most importantly, Xente comes with a robust workflow that enables users to initiate, approve, and track payments, as well as synchronize with accounting software. 

Professional businesses rely on Xente to handle, track, and report payment transactions, while also providing users with a world-class experience
