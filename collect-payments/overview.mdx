<Info>
  **Prerequisite** You need a Pro+ account to acccess Visa debit cards API. 
</Info>

With Xente, you can issue both physical and virtual Visa debit cards. 

Virtual Visa debit cards are ideal for online payments, such as SAAS payments and ad payments on websites like Meta, Google, Uber, and Microsoft, as well as for online shopping.

Physical Visa cards are perfect for offline payments and can be used to pay at any Visa-enabled point of sale worldwide or to withdraw cash at ATMs.
