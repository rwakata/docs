## Step 1 - POST mobile money collection transaction

You POST a mobile money transaction as below

```bash cURL
-H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_SECRET_KEY"
-X GET
```

## Step 3 - Receive Instant Payment Notification (IPN)


All transactions on Xente are asynchronous. Therefore, after you POST a mobile money collect transaction, you need to wait for a success (or failure) response using webhooks called Instant Payment Notifications (IPN). Once you receive a successful transaction, you can provision the service to your client.

Learn more about **[Instant Payment Notifications](/start/ipn)**.
