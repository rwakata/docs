With Xente, you get a virtual bank account. To receive funds via bank transfer, simply provide your virtual bank account details to the payer. 

They can transfer funds to your virtual bank account using EFT or RTGS. 

A unique feature of Xente is the ability to receive instant payment notifications (IPN) as soon as funds are deposited into your virtual bank account.

For further information on Instant Payment Notifications, please visit our [IPN documentation](/start/ipn).
